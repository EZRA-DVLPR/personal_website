Why I made this page

I often get asked what are my top 10 favorite songs and other similar questions. Thus, I have decided to compile this list as a means for an honest and accurate answer to such a question that pokes so dearly at one the subjects that I care for a lot. I have had a difficult time making the list and needed a way to get certain songs that don't make the cut to still be allowed a chance to be seen by others. This led to the creation of the Honorable Mentions list in addition to the original list of my Top 100 Songs.

I hope that this page is able to not only allow others to see what I think are great songs, but also so that these same people are able to look at songs that are hopefully different from those that they would normally listen to.
