## What is \_?



	- Just beat the fire boss. We have to beat the big boss that seems like BS... - 7/10/24
	- went ahead and finished the game. that boss was one of the easier bosses, just had to learn the timings. I still didn’t enjoy it though. The next boss was a pair of chimeras which was cool. the following boss was a blind boss who i abused by shooting arrows and attacking his backside. This was a fun battle. The Penetrator boss was the next one and he was a more of a standard souls boss and boy was he fun to fight against. not too challenging imo. Dirty colossus came next and he was super easy to defeat, just attack the backside and roll off the slugs or whatever. The next boss was the old monk and I didn’t like this one. He basically summons a hostile NPC to be the boss, which is a cool idea, but i got my ass handed to me. Luckily the 2nd attempt the guy who invaded left and i was able to fight the much easier NPC boss invader. After the monk came the storm king which was basically a larger flying manta ray. I think this one was funny but also kinda bs because you needed to use the weapon that one shots the smaller flying manta rays first, then hit him like 10 times with it. the hitbox was also very weird. overall easy boss though. The penultimate boss was of the night garl vinland and maiden astraea. Garl vinland was murdered and then the maiden killed herself so it wasn’t too eventful. The final boss was a 2 parter consisting of the blue dragon and then old king allant. The dragon I prepped and bought 450+ arrows to kill him. After i shot about 350 of them, the dragon died. Then i went to a bonfire and got my real setup to defeat the final boss of the game. Overall the final boss wasn’t that challenging either. Definitely some cool mechanics and attacks though. After that came the final post game boss and he was nothing but a trash mob. I was sad. Regardless, I beat the game, then went into NG+ with most of the worlds in white tendency. I then got the maiden so i can use the souls to level up. - 8/23/24
	- Overall, I really enjoyed the game. It is definitely reminiscent of the older style of games that have quirky boss fights and fun mechanics, which was enjoyable at times. I still dont like the dragon king boss though. The game did feel pretty easy overall though. The hardest part was the early game when I was unsure of what build and stuff to do.
